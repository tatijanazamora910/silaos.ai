About SilaOS

You started this business because you're good at what you do.
Not because you wanted to manage chaos.

SilaOS exists for the people buried in the business of running a business — and ready to get back to the work they actually love.

Who this is for

Real people. Real problems. Every single day.

We've been in these businesses. We've seen what happens when a call goes unanswered, when a new hire has no playbook, when the owner becomes the bottleneck of everything they built.

The master plumber who can't manage leads. The pilates studio owner buried in scheduling. The doctor whose front desk is overwhelmed. The HVAC tech who misses a call because he's under a unit — and loses the job before he even knows it existed.

These aren't small problems. They're the difference between a business that grows and one that grinds its owner into the ground.

The plumber who can't manage leads

Someone calls while he's on a job. They don't leave a message. He never follows up. That's revenue walking out the door — and he doesn't even know it happened.

The pilates studio owner buried in scheduling

Back-and-forth texts, last-minute cancellations, no-shows. She's spending hours a week on logistics that should take minutes. That's not why she opened a studio.

The doctor whose front desk is overwhelmed

Phones ringing, patients waiting, forms piling up. The team is doing their best. But the system underneath isn't keeping up. Patients feel it. Revenue reflects it.

The business owner who is the bottleneck

Every decision runs through them. Every problem lands on their desk. They can't grow what they can't get out of — and they know it. They just don't have a way out yet.

The name
Sila.

Force. Strength. Power.

Sila means strength — but not the loud kind. It's the kind that runs in the background while you're on the job, handles what you can't, and never needs credit for it.

That's what we built.

OS stands for operating system — because SilaOS isn't a single tool. It's the full system that runs the operational layer of your business. The layer that used to run through you.

What we believe

The principles behind everything we build.

01

People should do what only they can do

The plumber's skill is in the pipes. SilaOS handles everything that doesn't require that skill — so they can be fully present for everything that does.

02

A missed call is a real person who needed help

Behind every unanswered call is someone with a leaky pipe, a scheduling conflict, a health question, a need. SilaOS treats every missed call as what it actually is — an opportunity to help someone and serve them well.

03

AI should integrate, not replace

SilaOS fills the gaps — the after-hours calls, the follow-ups, the scheduling chaos — without replacing what's already working.

04

Every customer deserves to be heard in their language

The customer who calls in Spanish deserves the same response time and quality as the one who calls in English. SilaOS works in 100+ languages — because good service shouldn't have a language barrier.

05

Owners deserve to own their businesses

Not just in name — in reality. The business should be able to run without them being everywhere at once. SilaOS is built to make that possible — not someday, but now.

06

Nothing goes live unproven

Studio is the simulation environment. AI agents play the other side — the frustrated customer, the price-shopper, the after-hours emergency. Employees train individually. Teams train together. Before launch and every day after. Going live should feel like a decision, not a gamble.
