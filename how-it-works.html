The setup

Three steps. Then it's done.

We connect SilaOS to your phone number, your calendar, and your booking system. You don't need to be technical — we handle the integration. Most businesses are fully connected in under 15 minutes.

Works with your existing CRM, scheduling software, and booking system — whatever you're already using. Your phone number stays the same. SilaOS works in the background when you can't answer.

2

Build your Blueprint

Your pricing. Your protocols. Your communication standards. How you handle edge cases. What you take — and what you turn down. You write your rules once. SilaOS runs from them forever — and so does your team.

This is what makes SilaOS different. Not generic AI — your AI. A new hire joins? SilaOS answers their questions from your Blueprint. A customer asks about pricing? SilaOS responds your way. Write it once. It runs everything.

Go back to your craft

Once everything is built, tested, and trained — SilaOS runs in the background. Every morning you get a daily briefing: calls handled, jobs booked, revenue recovered. You step in only when your judgment — and only your judgment — is actually needed.

You're always in control. SilaOS surfaces what matters and handles what doesn't. You never lose visibility — you just stop being the person everything runs through.
