A few honest notes

What SilaOS is — and what it isn't.

SilaOS works alongside your existing tools — your CRM, your scheduling software, your dispatch system. It doesn't replace them. It fills the gap they all leave open: the moment a call goes unanswered and the lead disappears.

✕

Businesses with no inbound phone calls — SilaOS is built around the moment a call comes in and nobody picks up. No calls, no gap to fill.

✕

Owners who need to personally handle every client interaction — SilaOS is designed to take things off your plate. If nothing should ever happen without you, it won't fit.

✕

Businesses looking to replace their CRM, dispatch, or invoicing system — SilaOS is the missed-call layer that plugs in on top of whatever you already use. If you need a full platform replacement, that's not us.
